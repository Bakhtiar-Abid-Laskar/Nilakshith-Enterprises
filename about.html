Our Story

Built for Barak Valley

Nilakshith Enterprise was founded with a simple mission: bring world-class connectivity and IT infrastructure to every home and business in Barak Valley — regardless of size or budget.

Over the past decade, we've grown from a small local ISP into a full-service technology partner. Today, we offer broadband internet, professional CCTV installation, and complete IT networking under one roof — all delivered by the same tight-knit team that has been serving this community from the beginning.

We're based right here in Silchar. When you call us, you reach someone local — not a call center. When you need a technician, they're in your neighborhood. That's the Nilakshith difference.
