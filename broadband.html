FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the installation free?

Yes, installation is free within Silchar city limits. A nominal charge may apply for areas outside the city — contact us to confirm your location.

Are the plans unlimited?

Yes. All plans come with unlimited data — no FUP (Fair Use Policy) limits. Stream, download, and work as much as you need.

How long does installation take?

Most installations are completed within 2–4 hours on the same day as the survey, depending on cable length and building structure.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or change your plan at any time with a simple call or WhatsApp message. Plan changes typically take effect within 24 hours.

Do you provide support outside business hours?

Our official hours are Mon–Sat, 9 AM to 7 PM. For urgent outages, you can WhatsApp us and we'll assess the situation and respond as quickly as possible.
